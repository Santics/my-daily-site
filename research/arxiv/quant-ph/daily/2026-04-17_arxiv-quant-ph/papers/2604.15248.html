IQP circuits for 2-Forrelation

arXiv ID: 2604.15248

原链接: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15248

PDF: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15248

发布时间: 2026-04-16

作者: Quentin Buzet, André Chailloux

分类: quant-ph, cs.CC

备注: 27 pages

原摘要

The 2-Forrelation problem provides an optimal separation between classical and quantum query complexity and is also the problem used for separating BQP and PH relative to an oracle. A natural question is therefore to ask what are the minimal quantum resources needed to solve this problem. We show that 2-Forrelation can be solved using Instantaneous Quantum Polynomial-time (IQP) circuits, a restricted model of quantum computation in which all gates commute. Concretely, two IQP circuits with two quantum queries and efficient classical processing suffice. For the signed variant of 2-Forrelation, even a single IQP circuit and query suffices. This answers a recent open question of Girish (arXiv:2510.06385) on the power of commuting quantum computations.

We use this to show that (BPP^IQP)^O ⊄ PH^O relative to an oracle O, strengthening the result of Raz and Tal (STOC 2019). Our results show that IQP circuits can be used for classically hard decision problems, thus providing a new route for showing quantum advantage with IQP circuits, avoiding the verification difficulties associated with sampling tasks. 核心物理图像

2-Forrelation 问题是经典与量子查询复杂度分离的最优例子，也是证明 BQP 与 PH（多项式层级）相对分离的关键问题。传统上认为解决这类问题需要通用量子计算能力。本文发现，仅需 IQP 电路（一种所有门都对易的受限量子计算模型）即可解决 2-Forrelation，大幅降低了展示量子优势所需的资源门槛。

关键术语

  • IQP (Instantaneous Quantum Polynomial-time)：瞬时量子多项式时间，一种受限量子计算模型，所有门相互对易，可视为"同时"执行
  • 2-Forrelation：判断两个布尔函数是否相关的判定问题，经典查询复杂度 Ω(√N)，量子查询复杂度 O(1)
  • BQP：有界错误量子多项式时间，量子计算的复杂度类
  • PH：多项式层级，经典计算的复杂度类层次结构

主要贡献

  • 证明 2-Forrelation 可用 IQP 电路求解，解决 Girish (arXiv:2510.06385) 提出的开放问题
  • 仅需两个 IQP 电路和两个量子查询即可求解 2-Forrelation
  • 有符号变体甚至只需单个 IQP 电路和单个查询
  • 证明 (BPP^IQP)^O ⊄ PH^O 相对分离，强化 Raz-Tal (STOC 2019) 结果
  • 为展示量子优势提供新途径，避开采样任务的验证困难
