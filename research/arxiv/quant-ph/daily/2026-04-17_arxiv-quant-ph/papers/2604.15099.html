O3LS: Optimizing Lattice Surgery via Automatic Layout Searching and Loose Scheduling

arXiv ID: 2604.15099

原链接: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15099

PDF: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15099

发布时间: 2026-04-16

作者: Chenghong Zhu, Xian Wu, Jiahan Chen, Keming He, Junjie Wu, Xin Wang, Lingling Lao

分类: quant-ph

备注: Accepted by ISCA 2026

原摘要

Toward the large-scale, practical realization of quantum computing, quantum error correction is essential. Among various quantum error-correcting codes, the surface code stands out as a leading candidate, and lattice surgery based on surface codes has emerged as a promising technique for fault-tolerant quantum computation (FTQC). However, implementing quantum algorithms using lattice surgery introduces both resource and time overhead. Existing approaches typically focus on large layout designs, with compiler passes aimed primarily at optimizing time overhead. This often overlooks the trade-off between rotation bottlenecks and movement distance, which leads to inefficient resource utilization and prevents further reduction of the quantum computation failure rate.

To address these challenges, we introduce O3LS, a framework for optimizing lattice surgery through automatic layout search and loose scheduling. O3LS achieves an optimal balance by automatically generating squeezed data layouts to reduce space requirements and employing loose scheduling algorithms combined with circuit synthesis techniques to reduce time overhead, thereby effectively minimizing overall logical error rates. Numerical results indicate that O3LS can reduce space overhead by 28.0% over standard layouts and 46.7% over sparse layouts without increasing the number of time steps, leading to suppression of logical error rates by up to 16% relative to larger data layout designs. O3LS can also achieve time overhead reductions of 36.07% and 24.76% in compact and standard data layout designs, respectively. It suppresses logical error rates by up to an order of magnitude compared to prior compilers that focus primarily on maximizing parallelism.

核心物理图像

晶格手术是实现容错量子计算的关键技术，但传统编译器在优化时往往只关注时间开销，忽视了旋转瓶颈与移动距离之间的权衡，导致资源利用低效。O3LS 通过自动布局搜索找到压缩的数据布局，同时采用宽松调度算法平衡并行性与资源约束，从而在时空两个维度上同时优化。

关键术语

  • Lattice Surgery：基于表面码的容错量子计算技术，通过合并和分割逻辑量子比特实现逻辑门操作
  • Squeezed Data Layout：压缩的数据布局，减少空间开销的同时保持纠错能力
  • Loose Scheduling：宽松调度，允许一定程度的串行执行以换取更好的资源利用
  • Surface Code：表面码，具有高通门阈值和局部校验子的量子纠错码

主要贡献

  • 提出 O3LS 框架，实现自动布局搜索与宽松调度的联合优化
  • 相比标准布局减少 28% 空间开销，相比稀疏布局减少 46.7%
  • 在紧凑和标准布局中分别减少 36.07% 和 24.76% 时间开销
  • 逻辑错误率相比传统并行最大化编译器降低一个数量级

研究方法

采用自动布局搜索生成优化的数据布局，结合宽松调度算法和电路合成技术。通过数值模拟验证在标准、紧凑和稀疏三种布局配置下的性能提升。

结论与开放问题

O3LS 为晶格手术编译优化提供了实用框架，但当前评估基于模拟数据。开放问题包括：该方法在不同量子硬件（如中性原子、超导）上的适应性如何？动态布局调整在实时编译中的可行性？
