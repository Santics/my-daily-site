A unified framework for efficient quantum simulation of nonlinear spectroscopy

arXiv: 2604.16164 发布: 2026-04-17 类型: Algorithm 相关性: High

作者

Long Xiong, Xiaoyang Wang, Xiaoxia Cai, Xiao Yuan

原摘要

Nonlinear spectroscopy is a cornerstone of quantum science, providing unique access to multi-point correlations, quantum coherence, and couplings that are invisible to linear methods. However, classical simulation of these phenomena is fundamentally limited by the exponential growth of the Hilbert space, and practical quantum algorithms for the nonlinear regime have remained largely unexplored. Here, we present a unified quantum algorithmic framework for computing $n$-th order nonlinear spectroscopies. By reformulating multi-time responses as a weighted sum of expectation values at finite pump amplitudes via a generalized parameter shift rule, our approach bypasses the costly evaluation of high-order commutators and time-dependent operator expansions. This reformulation enables efficient execution via real-time evolution on current quantum hardware, ensuring inherent noise resilience. We validate the framework on IBM's superconducting quantum processors, successfully obtain higher-order response functions of a 12-qubit XXZ spin-chain.
