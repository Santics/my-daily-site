2604.20108 — Efficient Quantum Algorithms for Higher-Order Coupled Oscillators

arXiv ID: 2604.20108 | 作者: Caesnan M. G. Leditto, Angus Southwell, Muhammad Usman et al. | 发布时间: 2026-04-22 | 分类: quant-ph, math.DS, nlin.AO, physics.soc-ph

原摘要

Higher-order networks with multiway interactions can exhibit collective dynamical phenomena that are absent in traditional pairwise network models. However, analyzing such dynamics becomes computationally prohibitive as their state space grows combinatorially in the multiway interaction order. Here we develop quantum algorithms for two central tasks -- synchronization estimation and certification of the no-phase-locking regime -- in the simplicial Kuramoto model. This model is a higher-order generalization of the classical Kuramoto model where oscillators interact through multiway couplings on simplicial complexes. We show that a quantum computer can estimate the order parameter of the synchronization transition with a quadratic speedup over classical methods, and can certify the absence of phase-locking with an exponential speedup. Our results demonstrate that quantum algorithms can efficiently probe collective dynamics in complex systems with higher-order interactions, opening a new avenue for quantum-enhanced network science.

一句话核心问题

如何利用量子算法高效分析高阶网络中多体耦合振子的同步动力学行为？

核心物理图像

想象一个由许多振子组成的复杂网络，其中振子之间的相互作用不限于两两配对，而是可以三个人、四个人同时耦合。这种高阶相互作用使得网络能展现出传统配对模型无法描述的集体动力学现象。经典计算机在分析这类系统时，状态空间随相互作用阶数指数增长，很快变得不可计算。而量子计算机可以利用量子叠加态同时编码多个网络构型，通过量子测量提取同步序参量，从而以多项式或指数级优势完成经典计算机无法完成的任务。

主要贡献

（1）为 simplicial Kuramoto 模型的同步估计任务设计了量子算法，实现了相对于经典方法的二次加速；（2）为无相位锁定状态的认证任务设计了量子算法，实现了指数级加速；（3）建立了量子计算与高阶网络动力学之间的理论桥梁，为量子增强网络科学开辟了新方向。
