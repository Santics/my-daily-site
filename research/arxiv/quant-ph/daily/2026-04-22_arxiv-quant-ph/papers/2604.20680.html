Controllable non-Hermitian topology in a dynamically protected cat qubit

arXiv ID: 2604.20680 | 发布时间: 2026-04-22 | 分类: quant-ph | 作者: Tian-Le Yang, Pei-Rong Han, Zhen-Biao Yang, Shi-Biao Zheng
quantum-error-correctionphotonicopen-quantum-systems

原摘要

Dissipatively stabilized cat qubits are promising for fault-tolerant quantum information processing, yet their non-Hermitian (NH) spectral topology remains largely unexplored. We uncover rich Liouvillian exceptional structures in a cat-qubit mode stabilized by two-photon drive (TPD) and engineered two-photon loss, in the presence of single-photon drive (SPD) and single-photon loss. In the parameter space spanned by SPD strength and detuning, we identify both second- and third-order Liouvillian exceptional points (LEP2s and LEP3s). Remarkably, we show that the phase $θ$ of TPD provides coherent control over these exceptional points: the LEP3 diverges and vanishes at $θ=π/2$, while remaining stable and tunable elsewhere. 一句话核心问题

如何在耗散稳定的cat量子比特中，利用非厄米拓扑实现可控的Liouvillian异常点操控？

核心物理图像

cat量子比特通过双光子驱动（TPD）和工程化双光子损耗在耗散中被稳定。当系统中同时存在单光子驱动（SPD）和单光子损耗时，Liouvillian谱会出现异常点——即本征值和本征向量同时简并的特殊点。通过调节TPD的相位θ，可以控制三阶异常点（LEP3）的出现与消失：在θ=π/2时LEP3发散消失，在其他区域则保持稳定且可调。

主要贡献

（1）在cat量子比特模型中首次发现二阶和三阶Liouvillian异常点（LEP2和LEP3）的共存；（2）提出通过双光子驱动相位θ实现对异常点的相干控制方案，LEP3在θ=π/2时发散消失；（3）引入基于绕数的新拓扑不变量来表征异常点的拓扑性质，为开放量子系统的拓扑分类提供了新工具。
