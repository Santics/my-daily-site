Towards Ultra-High-Rate Quantum Error Correction with Reconfigurable Atom Arrays

arXiv ID: 2604.16209

原链接: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.16209

PDF: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.16209

发布时间: 2026-04-17

作者: Chen Zhao, Casey Duckering, Andi Gu, Nishad Maskara, Hengyun Zhou

分类: quant-ph, cs.IT

备注: Ultra-high-rate LDPC codes for neutral atom arrays

原摘要

Quantum error correction is widely believed to be essential for large-scale quantum computation, but the required qubit overhead remains a central challenge. Quantum low-density parity-check codes can substantially reduce this overhead through high-rate encodings, yet finite-size instances with practical logical error rates often achieve encoding rates only around or below 1/10. Here, building on a recent ultra-high-rate construction by Kasai, we identify new structural conditions on the underlying affine permutation matrices that make encoding rates exceeding 1/2 compatible with efficient implementation on reconfigurable neutral atom arrays. These conditions define a co-designed family of ultra-high-rate quantum codes that supports efficient syndrome extraction and atom rearrangement under realistic parallel control constraints. Using a hierarchical decoder with high accuracy and good throughput, we study the performance under a circuit-level noise model with p=0.1%, achieving per-logical-per-round error rates of 1.3_{-0.9}^{+3.0} × 10^{-13} with a [[2304,1156,≤ 14]] code and 2.9_{-1.5}^{+3.1} × 10^{-11} with a [[1152,580,≤ 12]] code. These results approach the teraquop regime, highlighting the promise of this code family for practical ultra-high-rate quantum error correction.

AI 解读

核心物理图像

量子纠错是实现大规模量子计算的必经之路，但现有纠错码的编码率往往很低（~1/10），导致巨大的量子比特开销。本文针对中性原子可重构阵列平台，设计了一种"超高比率"量子 LDPC 码族，编码率超过 1/2，同时保证在真实原子阵列控制约束下的高效实现。

关键思想是：通过识别仿射置换矩阵上的特定结构条件，使超高比率编码与原子阵列的并行控制、重排操作、综合征提取兼容。这是一种"协同设计"思路——不是先设计码再找硬件映射，而是让码的结构本身就适合原子阵列的操作约束。

关键术语

  • LDPC (Low-Density Parity-Check) codes：低密度奇偶校验码，具有局部校验子结构的量子纠错码
  • 编码率 (Code Rate)：k/n，逻辑量子比特数与物理量子比特数之比，反映纠错开销
  • Teraquop：万亿次量子操作（10^12 operations），纠错性能的常用指标
  • 仿射置换矩阵 (Affine Permutation Matrices)：LDPC 码的校验矩阵构造单元
  • 综合征提取 (Syndrome Extraction)：测量校验子以检测错误的操作
  • 原子重排 (Atom Rearrangement)：中性原子阵列中移动原子以形成所需几何构型的操作

主要贡献

  • 识别使编码率 >1/2 与中性原子阵列控制约束相容的结构条件
  • 提出协同设计的超高比率量子 LDPC 码族
  • [[2304,1156,≤14]] 码在 p=0.1% 下达到 1.3 × 10^{-13} 逻辑错误率
  • [[1152,580,≤12]] 码达到 2.9 × 10^{-11} 逻辑错误率
  • 结果接近 teraquop regime，展示实用超高比率量子纠错的前景

研究方法

基于 Kasai 的超高比率 LDPC 构造框架，分析仿射置换矩阵上的组合条件。设计分层解码器，在电路级噪声模型下进行蒙特卡洛模拟。考虑原子阵列的并行移动约束、测量约束和重排约束。
