Design and Analysis of Quantum Dual-Containing CSS LDPC Codes based on Quasi-Dyadic Matrices

作者: Alessio Baldelli, Marco Baldi, Massimo Battaglioni, Franco Chiaraluce, Paolo Santini
分类: cs.IT, quant-ph
发布时间: 2026-05-05 | 数据来源: arXiv
quantum-error-correctioncss-ldpc-codesquasi-dyadic-matricesdual-containing-codesbelief-propagation-decoding

原摘要

Building scalable quantum computers requires quantum error-correcting codes that enable reliable operations in the presence of noise. Motivated by such need, this paper introduces two constructions of high-rate, quantum dual-containing (DC) Calderbank-Shor-Steane (CSS) low-density parity-check (LDPC) codes based on quasi-dyadic matrices. Their DC structure enables the transversal implementation of the Hadamard gate, and, jointly with the sparsity of their parity-check matrices enable low-complexity decoding via a standard binary belief-propagation algorithm. We provide several theoretical results concerning the cycle properties of these CSS codes. We also investigate their automorphism groups as well as their minimum distance. Furthermore, through numerical simulations, we show that the quantum CSS LDPC codes obtained through these constructions achieve better finite-length误码率性能 than existing DC codes across different block lengths and code rates.
