Neural optimization for quantum architectures: graph embedding problems with Distance Encoder Networks

原摘要

Quantum machines are among the most promising technologies expected to provide significant improvements in the following years. However, bridging the gap between real-world applications and their implementation on quantum hardware is still a complicated task. One of the main challenges is to represent through qubits (i.e., the basic units of quantum information) the problems of interest. According to the specific technology underlying the quantum machine, it is necessary to implement a proper representation strategy, generally referred to as embedding. This paper introduces a neural-enhanced optimization framework to solve the constrained unit disk problem, which arises in the context of qubits positioning for neutral atoms-based quantum hardware. The proposed approach involves a modified autoencoder model, i.e., the Distances Encoder Network, and a custom loss, i.e., the Embedding Loss Function, respectively, to compute Euclidean distances and model the optimization constraints. The core idea behind this design relies on the capability of neural networks to approximate non-linear transformations to make the Distances Encoder Network learn the spatial transformation that maps initial non-feasible solutions of the constrained unit disk problem into feasible ones. The proposed approach outperforms classical solvers, given fixed comparable computation times, and paves the way to address other optimization problems through a similar策略.

核心物理图像

中性原子量子处理器中，原子被光镊捕获并排列在二维平面上。由于原子间的相互作用范围有限，量子门只能在距离足够近的原子对之间执行。约束单位圆盘问题（CUDP）要求将代表量子比特的原子放置在平面上，使得相互作用范围内的原子对形成所需的量子电路连接图，同时满足最小间距约束（避免原子重叠或相互作用过强）。这是一个组合几何优化问题，经典求解器在大规模下效率低下。本文提出的Distance Encoder Network是一种改进的自编码器，它学习一种非线性空间变换：将随机初始化的原子位置（通常不可行，即存在重叠或缺失连接）映射到满足所有约束的可行排布。Embedding Loss Function同时惩罚原子间距离过小（违反最小间距）和距离过大（缺失必要连接），引导网络收敛到高质量解。

主要贡献

本文的主要贡献包括：第一，将中性原子量子硬件中的量子比特排布问题形式化为约束单位圆盘问题（CUDP），明确了该问题在量子架构优化中的核心地位；第二，提出了Distance Encoder Network，一种改进的自编码器架构，利用神经网络的非线性逼近能力学习从不可行解到可行解的空间变换；第三，设计了Embedding Loss Function，同时编码欧氏距离计算和约束优化目标，使网络在训练过程中自动满足物理约束；第四，实验证明在固定计算时间内，该方法超越了经典求解器的性能，为大规模量子架构优化提供了可扩展的神经网络方案；第五，指出该框架可推广到其他量子架构优化问题。
