CAbLECAR: efficiently scheduling QLDPC codes on a tileable spin qubit chip with shuttling

arXiv ID: 2604.24739v1 PDF: 下载
作者: Jason D. Chadwick, Frederic T. Chong
分类: quant-ph, cs.ET
发布时间: 2026-04-27 | 数据来源: arXiv
quantum-error-correctionqldpc-codessemiconductor-spin-qubitsshuttling-architecture

原摘要

Semiconductor spin qubits are a promising platform for large-scale quantum computing, but have yet to take full advantage of the broad class of quantum low-density parity check (QLDPC) codes, which promise high encoding rates and efficient logic but require nonlocal connectivity between physical qubits. In this work, we investigate the implementation of QLDPC codes on a tileable, shuttling-based spin qubit architecture. By tailoring syndrome extraction circuits to the shuttling noise model, we significantly improve on previous surface code proposals and extend the feasible shuttling range of the architecture by 5-10x, enabling the implementation of more complex codes with长程 interactions. Taking inspiration from the field of robotics, we develop a coordinated shuttle scheduling algorithm that supports arbitrary codes and use it to benchmark the logical performance of a variety of promising code families. We find that the optimized schedules are up to 86% faster than hand-optimized schedules for certain code families. Through detailed circuit-level simulations, we identify specific QLDPC codes that improve upon prior surface code implementations by orders of magnitude, increasing encoding efficiency and reducing logical error rates. This work demonstrates the potential of shuttling-based spin qubit hardware platforms for scalable and efficient fault-tolerant quantum computation.

一句话核心问题

如何在可平铺的 shuttling 自旋量子比特芯片上高效调度 QLDPC 量子纠错码，并超越表面码的性能？

AI快评

本文系统研究了 QLDPC 码在 shuttling 型半导体自旋量子比特架构上的实现方案。作者针对 shuttling 噪声模型定制了 syndrome extraction 电路，将可行 shuttling 范围扩展了 5-10 倍，从而支持具有长程相互作用的更复杂编码。借鉴机器人领域的协调调度思想，作者开发了支持任意编码家族的自动 shuttle 调度算法，发现优化后的调度方案比手工优化快达 86%。通过电路级模拟，作者识别出特定 QLDPC 码在编码效率和逻辑错误率上比表面码提升数个数量级。这项工作为半导体自旋量子比特平台实现可扩展容错量子计算展示了明确的技术路径和性能优势。

核心物理图像

想象一个由多个平铺单元组成的半导体量子芯片，每个单元内有若干自旋量子比特。当需要进行非局域的 syndrome extraction 时，量子比特不是被动等待，而是像工厂里的自动导引车一样，通过协调的 shuttling 操作主动移动到目标位置完成相互作用。研究人员借鉴机器人路径规划算法，为整个芯片的量子比特编排了一套高效的移动调度方案——某些编码家族的调度速度比人工设计快了 86%。最终，特定的 QLDPC 码在这片可扩展的芯片上展现出比传统表面码高出数个数量级的纠错效率。

主要贡献

将 QLDPC 码引入 shuttling 型半导体自旋量子比特架构，开发了自动 shuttle 调度算法使性能比手工优化提升 86%，并通过电路级模拟证明特定 QLDPC 码在编码效率和逻辑错误率上超越表面码数个数量级，为可扩展容错量子计算提供了新的硬件-编码协同设计方案。
