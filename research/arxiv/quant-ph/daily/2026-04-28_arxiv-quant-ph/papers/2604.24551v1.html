GSC-QEMit: A Telemetry-Driven Hierarchical Forecast-and-Bandit Framework for Adaptive Quantum Error Mitigation

作者: Steven Szachara, Sheeraja Rajakrishnan, Dylan Jay Van Allen, Jason Pollack, Travis Desell, Daniel Krutz
分类: quant-ph, cs.LG
发布时间: 2026-04-27 | 数据来源: arXiv

原摘要

Quantum error mitigation (QEM) is essential for extracting reliable results from near-term quantum devices, yet practical deployments must balance mitigation强度 against runtime overhead under time-varying noise. We introduce GSC-QEMit, a telemetry-driven, context-forecast-bandit framework for adaptive mitigation that switches between lightweight suppression and heavier intervention as drift evolves. GSC-QEMit composes three coupled modules: (G) a Growing Hierarchical Self-Organizing Map (GHSOM) that clusters streaming telemetry into operating contexts; (S) an uncertainty-aware subsampled Gaussian-process forecaster that predicts short-horizon fidelity degradation; and (C) a cost-aware contextual multi-armed bandit (CMAB) that selects mitigation actions via Thompson sampling with explicit intervention cost. We evaluate GSC-QEMit on benchmark circuit families (GHZ, Quantum Fourier Transform, and Grover search) under nonstationary noise regimes simulated in Qiskit Aer, using an instrumented testbed where action labels correspond to graded mitigation intensity. Across Clifford, non-Clifford, and structured workloads, GSC-QEMit improves average logical fidelity by +9.0% relative to unmitigated execution while reducing unnecessary heavy interventions by reserving them for inferred noise spikes. The resulting policies exhibit a favorable fidelity-cost trade-off and transfer across the evaluated workloads without circuit-specific tuning.

AI快评

本文提出了 GSC-QEMit，一个遥测驱动的自适应量子错误缓解框架。该系统通过三个耦合模块实现：GHSOM 聚类遥测数据到操作上下文、高斯过程预测器预测短期保真度退化、上下文多臂老虎机通过 Thompson 采样选择缓解动作。在 Qiskit Aer 模拟的非平稳噪声环境下，GSC-QEMit 相比未缓解执行将平均逻辑保真度提高了 9.0%，同时减少了不必要的重度干预。这项工作将机器学习技术引入量子错误缓解的动态调度，对 NISQ 时代的实用化量子计算有重要价值，但仅在模拟环境中验证，需要真实量子硬件上的测试。

核心物理图像

想象一台量子计算机正在运行，但它的量子比特不断受到环境噪声的干扰，就像一台精密的天平在风中摇晃。传统的错误缓解方法像是固定开启一台强力除湿机——无论当前湿度如何，它都以最大功率运行，消耗大量电力。而 GSC-QEMit 就像一个智能空调系统：它首先通过传感器（遥测数据）识别当前环境状态（GHSOM 聚类），然后预测接下来几分钟的湿度变化趋势（高斯过程预测），最后决定是轻微通风还是强力除湿（多臂老虎机决策）。当噪声突然增大时，系统立即启动强力缓解；当环境稳定时，则切换为轻量模式节省资源。在 Qiskit 模拟中，这套智能系统将量子计算的保真度提升了 9%，同时避免了不必要的重度干预开销。

主要贡献

提出 GSC-QEMit 自适应量子错误缓解框架，结合 GHSOM 聚类、高斯过程预测和上下文多臂老虎机决策，在非平稳噪声模拟中实现平均逻辑保真度提升 9.0%，同时减少不必要的重度干预，展示了机器学习驱动的动态错误缓解在 NISQ 计算中的潜力。
